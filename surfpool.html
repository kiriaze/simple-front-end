WAVELOCH SURFPOOL “V” SERIES

Surf culture for all. WaveLoch’s V Series is our most economical SurfPool product. Able to be enjoyed by multiple surfers at a time, this wave breeds new and repeat business.

  • Wave Height: 1.5 meters (4.9 ft)
  • Wave Frequency/Period: 7.5 seconds
  • Length of Ride: 10 seconds each wave
  • Break Direction: Left/Right
  • Break Count: 2–6 waves
  • Skill Level: Beginner to Intermediate
  • Pool Dimensions:
  • Single Reef: 50m (164’) by 100m (328’) and 3m (10’) max depth
  • Double Reef: 50m (164’) by 150m (492’) and 3m (10’) maximum depth
  • Triple Reef: 50m (164’) by 200m (656’) and 3m (10’) maximum depth
  • Multiple Reef Break Height Detail:
  • System Type: Vacuum
  • Energy Source: Electric or Natural Gas

WAVELOCH SURFPOOL “X” SERIES

WaveLoch’s X Series has all the capabilities of the V Series, with added pressurized caissons to generate a primary break of two, three, or even four meters high! These competition class waves are designed for the more seasoned surfer who wants to get barreled, test new maneuvers, and fly down the line. The X Series will bring competitive surfing to the masses and revolutionize how the sport of surfing evolves. The larger waves provided by the X Series are perfect for making the SurfPool the centerpiece attraction of a resort, real estate development, or sports center. Notwithstanding the larger wave generating capacity offered by the X Series, after its first break, the following second and third breaks perform identically to the V Series described above.

WaveLoch’s Keys to Success

HIGH WAVE FREQUENCY

  • Proprietary wave-generating system produces multiple surfable waves every 7.5 seconds (compared to our competitors’ 1 wave every 90 seconds).
  • Higher frequency results in greater customer throughput, maximizing the return on large pool capital investments.

SURF SCIENCE

  • Understanding the science of waves allows WaveLoch’s hydrodynamic computer and physical modeling systems to validate and enable customization to each unique customer location.

WATER SURFACE SMOOTHING

  • Contrary to traditional waterpark wave pools, which produce random chop and reflecting waves, a surfing pool is a closed system that inherently reflects any wave action off enclosure walls.
  • WaveLoch’s proprietary dampening systems eliminates adverse surface chop and rip currents while facilitating perfect wave formation and progressive breaking at multiple points down the length of the pool.

ENHANCED ENERGY EFFICIENCY

  • Energy usage is optimized through multiple reef design, the use of natural gas, and technology enhancements to power systems.

MULTIPLE REEFS

  • Wave output and ride count are maximized through strategic reef configurations—capable of producing multiple, 10 second long rides to simultaneously accommodate numerous surfers, each on their own wave.

PROVEN ECONOMIC RETURN

  • As an operator, WaveLoch understands that stable financial return is not only a function of revenue generated from the attraction, but factors in the attraction’s ability to hold a crowd and create ancillary food, beverage, and retail income, as well as hospitality room and real estate value.
  • WaveLoch, through its sister company Wave House, has a proven track record in operating wave attractions and has a verifiable database of historical financial performance to offer real-world economic feasibility comparables applicable to your site.
