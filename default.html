Waveloch
Pioneers of wave craft

Since 1991, wave loch has been on a mission to 'wave the planet' with a broad range of wave-making products.

Rooted in California's Surf Culture

WaveLoch was founded by surfer and entrepreneur, Tom Lochtefeld. His passion for surfing led to the invention of sheet wave technology, which fostered the sport of flowboarding via WaveLoch’s FlowRider® and FlowBarrel® systems. Lochtefeld also founded and developed the world famous Wave House®, the first commercially successful, stand-alone surfing lifestyle, en- tertainment, and restaurant business featuring WaveLoch’s sheet wave attractions.

WAVELOCH IS MUCH MORE THAN A CREATIVE TECHNOLOGY COMPANY.
WE ARE A LIFESTYLE COMPANY

We consider the beach not only our home, but our office, playground, and product muse. This deep connection to surfing as a lifestyle makes us the best at what we do because we embody all that our products represent. In addition to our first-hand experience in the design, development, and management of efficient and profitable surf parks, WaveLoch’s distinct business model builds on other sector-specific knowledge separating us from the competition.

WAVELOCH MAKES MORE THAN SENSE. WE MAKE DOLLARS.

With direct expertise in venue design, real estate development, construction, and operation of restaurants, retail, corporate events, and concerts, WaveLoch has a rare insight and understanding into the surf park business model as a whole. By harnessing these multi-disciplinary skills, WaveLoch will confidently guide you through the chop to ensure the success of your venue.
